#### 7. Simple Addition

Adding is when we put groups of things together to find out how many there are in total.

**Exercise:** 2 + 3 = ?

#### 8. Simple Subtraction

Subtraction is when we take away some things from a group to see how many are left.

**Exercise:** 5 - 2 = ?

**Answer:**
