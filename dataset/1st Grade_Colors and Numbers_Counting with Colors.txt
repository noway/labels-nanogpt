**What are Colors?**

Colors are all around us! When you look around, you can see blue sky, green trees, red apples, and yellow bananas. Colors help us describe and enjoy the world.

**What is Counting?**

Counting is the way we find out how many things there are. When you count, you go from one number to the next, starting with number 1.
