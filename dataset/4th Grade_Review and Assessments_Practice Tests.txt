## Practice Test 1 - Answer Key

### Multiple Choice

1. **B) 42**  
   Explanation: 7 × 6 equals 42.

2. **B) 3**  
   Explanation: If you start with 5 apples and give away 2, you subtract 2 from 5, which leaves you with 3 apples.

3. **B) 6**  
   Explanation: 48 divided by 8 is 6.

### True/False

4. **True**  
   Explanation: The number 456 is even because it ends with a 6, which is an even number.

5. **True**  
   Explanation: A square is a rectangle with all four sides of equal length.

### Fill in the Blanks

6. **250**  
   Explanation: 1000 - 250 = 750.

7. **72**  
   Explanation: There are 24 hours in one day. So, 24 × 3 = 72 hours in three days.

### Word Problems

8. **48 crayons**  
   Explanation: If one pack has 12 crayons and Sarah has 4 packs, multiply 12 × 4 to get 48 crayons.

9. **45 kilometers**  
   Explanation: If Ben rode 15 kilometers each day for 3 days, multiply 15 × 3 to get 45 kilometers.

10. **$1.00**  
   Explanation: Each pencil costs $0.25, so 4 pencils would cost 4 × $0.25 = $1.00.
