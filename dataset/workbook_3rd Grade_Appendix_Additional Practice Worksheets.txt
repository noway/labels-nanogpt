**Worksheet A5: Fractions Practice**

1. Write 1/2 as a decimal.
2. Which is bigger: 2/3 or 3/5?
3. What is the equivalent fraction of 4/6?
4. Add 3/8 + 1/4.
5. Subtract 5/6 from 1.

*Answer Key A5:*

1. 0.5
2. 2/3 is bigger than 3/5.
3. 2/3 is the equivalent fraction of 4/6.
4. 3/8 + 1/4 = 5/8.
5. 1 - 5/6 = 1/6.

**Worksheet A6: Geometry Practice**

1. How many sides does a triangle have?
2. What is the name of a four-sided figure?
3. How many corners does a rectangle have?
4. What shape has the same length and width?
5. Draw a shape with 5 sides.

*Answer Key A6:*

1. A triangle has 3 sides.
2. A four-sided figure is called a quadrilateral.
3. A rectangle has 4 corners.
4. A square has the same length and width.
5. A shape with 5 sides is called a pentagon. (drawing not included)

**Worksheet A7: Measurement Practice**

1. Which is longer: 1 foot or 1 meter?
2. How many centimeters are there in a meter?
3. If you have 2 quarters, 1 dime, and 2 pennies, how much money do you have in cents?
4. How many minutes are there in 2 hours?
5. If you have a ribbon that is 4 meters long and you cut it into 2 equal pieces, how long is each piece?

*Answer Key A7:*

1. 1 meter is longer than 1 foot.
2. There are 100 centimeters in a meter.
3. You have 57 cents (2 quarters = 50 cents, 1 dime = 10 cents, 2 pennies = 2 cents).
4. There are 120 minutes in 2 hours.
5. Each piece is 2 meters long.
