## Introduction

### Puzzle 1: Number Bonds to 10

🔍 **Find the Pairs!**

Can you find all the pairs of numbers that add up to 10?

Example: 3 + 7 = 10

Now you try! Write down all the pairs.

- 1 + __ = 10
- 2 + __ = 10
- __ + 6 = 10
- 4 + __ = 10
- __ + 8 = 10

**Answers:**

- 1 + 9 = 10
- 2 + 8 = 10
- 4 + 6 = 10
- 4 + 6 = 10
- 2 + 8 = 10

### Puzzle 2: Shape Counting

🔎 **Count the Sides!**

Look at the shapes below and count how many sides each shape has. Write the number of sides next to the shape.

Shapes:
- Triangle
- Rectangle
- Pentagon
- Hexagon
- Octagon

**Answers:**

- Triangle: 3 sides
- Rectangle: 4 sides
- Pentagon: 5 sides
- Hexagon: 6 sides
- Octagon: 8 sides

### Puzzle 4: Addition Mazes

🌟 **Find Your Way Through!**

Start at the top of the maze and find your way to the bottom by following the path of correct addition facts.

Maze 1:

Start at 5. Add 2 at each step.

(Start) → 5 → __ → __ → __ → (End)

Maze 2:

Start at 3. Add 3 at each step.

(Start) → 3 → __ → __ → __ → (End)

**Answers:**

Maze 1:

(Start) → 5 → 7 → 9 → 11 → (End)

Maze 2:

(Start) → 3 → 6 → 9 → 12 → (End)

### Puzzle 5: Telling Time Mystery

🕓 **Time Detective**

There are 3 clocks shown below, each showing a different time. Can you write the time shown on each clock?

Clock 1: ____

Clock 2: ____

Clock 3: ____

**Answers:**

Clock 1: 3:00 (Three o'clock)

Clock 2: 7:30 (Seven thirty, or half past seven)

Clock 3: 12:15 (Twelve fifteen, or a quarter past twelve)

### Puzzle 6: Subtraction Crossword

🔢 **Number Words**

Fill in the crossword with the correct number words that solve the subtraction problems.

1. 10 - 4 = ____
2. 9 - 5 = ____
3. 8 - 3 = ____
4. 6 - 6 = ____
5. 7 - 2 = ____

   1 2 3 4 5
   -----------
1 |S I X
2 |F O U R
3 |F I V E
4 |Z E R O
5 |F I V E

**Answers:**

1. 10 - 4 = **six (6)**
2. 9 - 5 = **four (4)**
3. 8 - 3 = **five (5)**
4. 6 - 6 = **zero (0)**
5. 7 - 2 = **five (5)**
