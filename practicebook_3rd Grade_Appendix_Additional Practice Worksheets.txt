#### Worksheet 4: Word Problems Using Four Operations

**4.1. Solve the following word problems:**

1. Anna has 27 apples and gives 9 to her friend. How many apples does Anna have left?  
   **Answer:** 27 - 9 = 18 apples

2. Barry has 10 packs of stickers. Each pack has 5 stickers. How many stickers does Barry have in all?  
   **Answer:** 10 × 5 = 50 stickers

3. Clara bought a book for $8. She gives the cashier $10. How much change should she get back?  
   **Answer:** $10 - $8 = $2 change

4. Darryl is three times as old as his sister, who is 7 years old. How old is Darryl?  
   **Answer:** 7 × 3 = 21 years old

5. There are 8 cars in the parking lot and 4 more cars arrive. How many cars are in the parking lot now?  
   **Answer:** 8 + 4 = 12 cars

#### Worksheet 5: Geometry and Shapes

**5.1 Identify the following shapes and provide their attributes:**

1. Square - 4 equal sides and 4 right angles.
2. Rectangle - Opposite sides are equal and 4 right angles.
3. Triangle - 3 sides and the sum of angles is 180 degrees.
4. Circle - Round shape, no corners, and continuous curve.
5. Hexagon - 6 sides and 6 angles.

**5.2 Count the number of sides for the following shapes and write it next to the shape name:**

1. Pentagon - **5 sides**
2. Octagon - **8 sides**
3. Triangle - **3 sides**
4. Hexagon - **6 sides**
5. Quadrilateral - **4 sides**
